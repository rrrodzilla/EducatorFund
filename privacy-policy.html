Educator Fund - Privacy Policy

At EducatorFund, we value our community. You trust us with your information, and we’re serious about that responsibility. We believe in transparency, and we’re committed to being upfront about our privacy practices, including how we treat personal information we collect or receive. We know you care about your privacy, too, so we provide settings that allow you to control how certain information is used on EducatorFund. This policy explains our privacy practices for EducatorFund.com (the “Site”), EducatorFund’s mobile application (the “App”) and services provided by Neyborly Company (collectively, “EducatorFund”). By visiting the Site or using the App, you are accepting the terms of this Privacy Policy. Any external links to other websites are clearly identifiable as such, and we are not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of these other websites.

Current version published: November 4, 2018 

Information Collected or Received

EducatorFund will not disclose your name, email address or other personal information to third parties without your explicit consent, except as specified in this policy.

As an EducatorFund member, you are required to provide a valid email address at registration and choose a username or alias that may represent your identity on EducatorFund.

Depending on which services you choose to use, EducatorFund may require additional information, such as a class and/or school name, billing and payment information (including billing address, telephone number, credit card information), a telephone number, and/or a physical postal address. As an educator, if you choose to receive payouts, EducatorFund may require information such as your social security number, identification ID or tax ID, date of birth, bank account information, credit card information and/or other proof of identification in order to verify your identity and provide this service to you. Credit card information on an account may be stored and used for both billing and payment purposes. EducatorFund may contact individual educators to confidentially request more information about their classes or items listed on the App, to ensure compliance with our rules. Certain products or services on EducatorFund may require you to complete an application; any information you submit through the application processes will not be displayed publicly and will only be used internally by EducatorFund unless otherwise specified.

You may optionally choose to provide your full name and other personal information (such as birthday, gender, location) to be publicly displayed in connection with your account and activity.

Your username, EducatorFund ID or alias is publicly displayed and connected to your EducatorFund activity. Other people may see your date joined, reviews, ratings, profile information, classes listed and comments in community spaces.

EducatorFund automatically receives and records some information from your browser or your mobile device when you visit the Site or use the App, such as your IP address or unique device identifier, cookies and data about which pages you visit on the Site or on the Apps. This information helps us analyze and understand how the Site and the App work for members and visitors, and provide a more personalized experience for members and visitors.

EducatorFund uses the information described in this policy to provide and improve our services and products, for billing and payments, for identification and authentication purposes, to contact members or interested parties, and for general research and aggregate reporting.

EducatorFund’s Terms of Use require all account owners to be at least 18 years of age (or have the permission and supervision of a responsible parent or legal guardian), therefore, this policy does not discuss the use of our Site, our Apps, or services by minors.

Choice & Control

We know members of our community value having control over their own information, and therefore EducatorFund gives you the choice of providing, editing or removing certain information, as well as the choice over how we contact you.

You may change or correct your EducatorFund account information (except your username) on the App. You may also remove certain optional information on the App that you no longer wish to be publicly visible, such as your full name.

For various reasons, EducatorFund may contact you about our services or your activity. Some of these messages are required, service-related messages for members. Other messages are not required, and you can control which optional messages you choose to receive by changing your settings in your account. Learn more in the Messages from EducatorFund section of this policy.

If you have optionally connected your account to an external, third-party application, such as Facebook, or an app developed using the EducatorFund API, you can remove permission for the app by changing your account settings.

If you no longer wish to use EducatorFund’s services or receive service-related messages, then you may close your account. Contact support@educatorfund.com to cancel your account.

Messages from EducatorFund

On occasion, EducatorFund will contact you. Primarily, messages are delivered by email or by text message, and every account is required to keep a valid email address on file to receive messages. In some situations, EducatorFund may contact you by telephone, SMS message or physical mail in order to provide you with customer support or to provide you with information about products and features you may find of interest. You may update your contact preferences in your Stripe account settings.

Some messages from EducatorFund are service-related and required for members. Examples of service-related messages include, but are not limited to: a welcome/confirmation email when you register your account, notification of an order, or correspondence with EducatorFund’s support team. These messages are not promotional in nature. You may not opt-out of receiving service-related messages from EducatorFund unless you close your account.

As a member, EducatorFund may also send you messages related to certain features or your activity on the Site or App. EducatorFund may also send you news or updates about changes to our Site, App, or services. By default, members will receive these messages via email or text messages through the App.

Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. Your EducatorFund account information is tied to your physical mobile device. It is important that you protect against unauthorized access of your account and information by choosing a password carefully for your device, and keeping your password and device secure at all times after using our services.  

EducatorFund encrypts sensitive information (such as credit card number) using secure socket layer technology (SSL).  Since EducatorFund works over text messaging, EducatorFund will never ask for sensitive information via text messaging.  In cases where EducatorFund needs sensitive information (such as a credit card number), the App will call your device to obtain your details securely.  EducatorFund follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once EducatorFund receives it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. EducatorFund offers optional advanced security settings for members,  full-site SSL, and usage history.
